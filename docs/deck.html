03 · The product

A structured deliberation,
not a group chat.

  • Independent analysis — each model works the problem alone, in separate research lanes, before seeing any other opinion.
  • Cross-examination — claims become a typed evidence graph; genuine disagreements are escalated and rebutted, not averaged away.
  • Chairman's verdict — go / no-go, a confidence score, the conditions under which it holds, and what would change the answer.

Every stage is schema-validated. Every call is on the cost receipt. Everything runs read-only.

04 · The proof

Measured, not vibes.

100% vs 77%

05 · Why it's different

No keys. No cloud.
No trust required.

No API keysOrchestrates the provider CLIs you already have installed and pay for. Zero new accounts, zero new bills.
Local & privateRuns entirely on your machine, bound to 127.0.0.1. Your idea never leaves your laptop.
Read-only, honestModels can't write or execute. Verdicts ship receipts — and follow-ups are labeled "1 call · no council" when the council didn't convene.
Adaptive costAuto mode routes a simple decision to a 1-call fast path and a hard one to a full council — you pay for deliberation only when it's needed.
