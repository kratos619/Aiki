# Authored pilot evidence — four-day support schedule

Twelve-week pilot ending 2026-06-30:

- 20 volunteer pilot agents and 20 matched comparison agents.
- Tickets per paid hour: pilot 9% higher.
- Median first response: pilot 18 minutes; comparison 17 minutes.
- Customer satisfaction: pilot 4.62; comparison 4.60.
- Backlog: pilot 3% higher.
- Sick days: pilot 28% lower.
- Voluntary departures: pilot 0; comparison 2.
- Coverage maintained using staggered schedules.
- Peak season excluded; supervisors partly selected for schedule fit; quality-audit error rates not recorded.

These are authored benchmark observations. They do not establish causality or jurisdiction-specific compliance.
