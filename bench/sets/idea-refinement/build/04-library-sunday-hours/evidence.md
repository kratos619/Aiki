# Authored pilot evidence — library access decision

As of 2026-06-15:

- Eight Sunday pilot days averaged 410 central-branch visits.
- Optional exit survey: 624 total responses; 61% reported no access during existing hours, 38% were caregivers
  with children, and 27% were shift workers. Categories may overlap.
- Average incremental Sunday cost: $3,650; 52 equivalent Sundays would cost $189,800 before contingency.
- Weekday literacy, job-search, and children's programs have 520 combined waitlisted applicants and a 72%
  completion rate.
- Pilot omitted winter and summer and collected no visitor-origin data.

These are authored benchmark observations. The optional survey is not a representative citywide sample.
